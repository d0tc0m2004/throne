How to Play

Win: Capture enemy King OR get your King to opponent's back row

Pieces:

  • King (K) - Moves 1 square any direction
  • Champion (C) - Moves like Queen, max 2 squares
  • Tower (T) - Moves like Rook, max 3 squares
  • Soldier (S) - Moves 1 square forward or sideways

Sacrifice: Once per game, sacrifice a piece for a powerful effect!
