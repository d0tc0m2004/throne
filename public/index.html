THRONE

A fast-paced tactical chess variant

How to Start a Game

  1. Player 1: Click "Create Room"
  2. Share the 4-letter code (e.g., AB3X) with your friend
  3. Player 2: Enter the code and click "Join Room"
  4. Game starts! White moves first.

The Board

Played on a 5x5 board - smaller than chess, but every move counts!

Win Conditions

Capture the King

Take your opponent's King piece

Reach the Throne

Get YOUR King to the opponent's back row

The Pieces

K King

Moves 1 square in any direction. Protect it, or race it to victory!

C Champion

Moves like a Queen, but max 2 squares. Your most powerful attacker.

T Tower

Moves like a Rook (straight lines), but max 3 squares.

S Soldier

Moves 1 square forward or sideways only. No backward, no diagonal.

The Sacrifice System

Once per game, sacrifice a piece for a powerful effect!

Sacrifice Soldier Champion moves twice this turn
Sacrifice Tower King immune for 1 turn
Sacrifice Champion Instantly kill any adjacent enemy

Strategy Tips

  • The King race is real - Sometimes attacking is the best defense!
  • Champions are valuable - Don't trade carelessly.
  • Soldiers can block - Use them to protect your King.
  • Save your sacrifice - The threat can be as powerful as using it.
  • Tower lanes - A Tower controls an entire row or column.

Controls

Click a piece - Select it (shows valid moves)

Green dots - Valid moves

Red squares - Capture moves

Blue glow - King is immune

Games are fast (5-10 minutes). Have fun!
