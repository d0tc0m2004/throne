How to Start a Game

  1. Player 1: Open the link and click "Create Room"
  2. You'll get a 4-letter code (e.g., AB3X) - share this with your friend
  3. Player 2: Open the same link, enter the code, and click "Join Room"
  4. Game starts automatically! White moves first.

Win Conditions

There are two ways to win:

Capture the King

Take your opponent's King piece, just like checkmate in chess.

Reach the Throne

Get YOUR King to the opponent's back row (White to row 0, Black to row 4).

The Pieces

K King

Moves 1 square in any direction (same as chess). Protect it, or race it to victory!

C Champion

Moves like a Queen, but max 2 squares. Your most powerful attacker.

T Tower

Moves like a Rook (straight lines), but max 3 squares. Great for controlling lanes.

S Soldier

Moves 1 square forward or sideways only. No backward, no diagonal. Simple but useful!

The Sacrifice System

Once Per Game - Use It Wisely!

Each player can use one sacrifice per game. Click the "Sacrifice Mode" button on your turn to activate one of these powerful effects:

Sacrifice Soldier → Champion Double Move
Remove one of your Soldiers from the board. Your Champion can move twice this turn!
Sacrifice Tower → King Shield
Remove your Tower from the board. Your King cannot be captured for one full turn (until your next turn).
Sacrifice Champion → Instant Kill
Remove your Champion from the board. Instantly eliminate any enemy piece adjacent to one of your pieces.

Strategy Tips

  • The King race is real - Sometimes attacking is the best defense. If your King is closer to their back row, they have to stop you!
  • Champions are valuable - Don't trade your Champion carelessly. It controls a lot of space on this small board.
  • Soldiers can block - Use them to protect your King or block enemy paths.
  • Save your sacrifice - The threat of a sacrifice can be as powerful as using it. Your opponent has to play around it.
  • Tower lanes - A Tower can control an entire row or column. Position it wisely.

Controls

  • Click a piece → Select it (shows valid moves)
  • Click a highlighted square → Move there
  • Green dots → Valid moves
  • Red squares → Capture moves
  • Blue glow on King → King is immune (sacrifice active)
